HOBBIES

Education is a part of life, but it's important that it's not the only thing. Study isn't everything, and for me whenever I'm not studying, or simply laying around, I decide to do one of the following activities, as they are something I enjoy dearly. Other than the activities listed below, I also enjoy outdoor recreation, rock climbing, and simply exploring the world we live in.
I have been playing chess since I was 6 years old, and I absolutely love this game. It's amazing how such a simple concept, and a 8x8 board can prove to be hours and hours of fun. I really love this game because it gets your mind going, and it easily kills time on a boring day.
Video games are the fad right now, and particularly LoL (League of Legends). I play this with my friends and whether we win or lose, we had fun regardless. This game not only is strategy, but also a team effort, and I will always be a team players whether I win or lose, and this is why I play.
Badminton has always been a sport that interests me. I like mostly all raquet sports, but this is one of my favourite. I like playing singles, doubles, and variations like king-of-the-ring. I haven't played this year alot, but I will try my best, as it's always a fun sport.
Swimming is one of the greatest activities ever. Just spending the time in the water with friends, diving off the boards, having breathings challenges, and even racing, are just few of the activities that you can do. Also, it's fun to splash water at my friends.
